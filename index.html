Guidelines for Students to access the Virtual Online Classes

The online Academic content of all subjects for all classes(Nursery to VIII) has been segregated into defined weeks.

Each Academic content will comprise:
  • An online lesson in the form of a video and/or Power Point Presentation on the related topic.
  • Assignment based on the topic for daily overview of the content.

Steps to gain access to the Virtual Online classes:
  • You may login from the student's login ID.
  • You will get an OTP on the active mobile number for login.
  • For each class, you will find subject wise links for videos, power points and assignments.

  • Students are required to view the academic content, take printouts of the assignments, solve them for better understanding of the delivered topic.
  • It is important to note that the device being used to gain access should be supported with - Video Player, MS Power Point, Adobe Reader, High speed internet connection and Head phones.
  • Raise a hand option is available to ask queries, during the doubt solving session.
  • Prepare notebooks for all the subjects and complete your assignment on daily basis in a neat and clean handwriting.
  • Create a regular study space and stay organized.
  • The best online students know how to lessen the distractions and set aside time to focus.
  • Actively Participate in the Virtual Online classes to help you better understand the course materials and engage with fellow classmates.
  • For Virtual Online classes, login of student should be done regularly and on the assigned time.

A Message from the Head of School - The International School of Bombay

Welcome to The International School of Bombay, a school where each child is valued and celebrated!

According to eminent sociologist respect, truth, and knowledge are the foundational elements for educating children. These values are at the heart of The International School of Bombay which educates its students and creates a socially, emotionally, and physically safe environment for the students to grow and learn.We understand that each student deserves a strong educational, social, and cultural foundation in order to navigate through life successfully.

Choosing the right school is one of the most important decisions you will ever make for your child. It is a decision that shapes the future of your child. We ensure that at The International School of Bombay, the education offers each child the opportunity to reach his/her greatest potential. Enrolling your child at The International School of Bombay is an investment that promises huge dividends in the years to come.

We will present your child with opportunities to sharpen the mind, expand academic knowledge, and to stretch intellectual reach, while developing skills as an independent thinker and confident communicator. This is a school that develops the whole child through exceptional academics , traditional values, enriching activities and a community of family and support.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we are very much careful about our student's safety and security.We are continuously working on providing children with information about how to protect themselves; promoting best handwashing and hygiene practices and providing hygiene supplies; cleaning and disinfecting school buildings, especially water and sanitation facilities; and increasing airflow and ventilation.

Our website is our online home, but there is something special about personally visiting our true home where the atmosphere is charged with the excitement of learning and where each student is fully engaged. 

I enthusiastically welcome you for a personal visit to our school so that you can experience firsthand "The International School of Bombay Magic" and appreciate the passion for a school that instills excellence and transforms generations.

 

Do come to see how fortunate I am to experience this joy, on a daily basis.I am committed to the success of my students…it is my passion!

In the spirit of academic excellence,

Shaifali Yadav
